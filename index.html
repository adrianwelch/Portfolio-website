About me

After completing my B.A. at The University of Chicago, I moved to Mexico City to start a company with an online portal that would guide Mexican students through the American college application process. I came into this project with an entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for education. After working closely as a stakeholder with our web developers, I quickly became fascinated by their ability to bring my ideas to life--it also sparked an interest in me to stay in the tech field and eventually learn how to execute these ideas myself.

Upon returning to the U.S., I worked at a tech startup in vacation rentals, but soonafter decided to enroll in a Web Development Immersive program with the intention to gain more technical skills and further my career in the tech industry. There, I devoted twelve hours a day to Javascript and Ruby coding challenges, elaborate assignments using Rails and Node, and projects that allowed me to practice what I enjoy most: solving problems and parsing them alongside colleagues that share the same motivation and enthusiasm to learn and constantly better a product.
